About The Organization

About a year ago I started working on projects in the small country of Nicaragua. It was then I discovered there was a vast amount of children in unfortunate situations. I knew I had an obligation to help in some form.

Since around february of last year we have helped around Six individual cases and a few hundred with our food campaigns. For every individual case we work on there are a plethora more in need of help in some form. Our goal is to mitigate the problem as much as we can. We want to be able to reach as many children as we can.
